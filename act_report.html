Introduction¶

This project analyses data from a Twitter account named @dog_rates. WeRateDogs is a Twitter account that rates people's dogs with a humorous comment about the dog.

WeRateDogs rates various images (i.e. the account does not rate only Dogs), for this report to create a trustworthy analyses; those ratings that were different from that of a dog were filtered out.

This Report highlights some insights & Visualization that were generated after the Data Wrangling process was completed.

Visualization¶

These are some insights that were generated from WeRateDogs Twitter Data.

The visualizations were structured to answer specific questions about Dogs and WeRateDogs account

Insights:¶

Which Dog breeds is the most loved judging by the Favorite Count?¶

The analysis shows us that if we are to judge by the favourite_counts of the WeRateDogs Twitter; we see visually that the top three Dogs are:

  1. Saluki (Known as sighthounds – hunting by sight more than scent or sound)
  2. French Bulldog (The head is square shaped and large, with ears that resemble bat ears with a flat-face.
  3. Afghan Hound (Distinguished by its thick, fine, silky coat and its tail with a ring curl at the end)

What sort of correlation exist between the Retweets_Counts & Favourite_counts?¶

The Scatter Plot above shows us that there is positive correlation between Favorite Count & Retweet Count. (this may mean that the higher the Favorite Count; the higher the Retweet Count)

We can not make conclusions solely on a Scatter Plot, therefore a Pearson correlation coefficient was calculated between both indicators; this shows us a 92% correlation (which is very strong).
